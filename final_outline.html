An analysis of Venezuela's food shortage and its dietary repercussions

Venezuelan non-profit Code for Venezuela has compiled a detailed dataset recording the diets of tens of thousands of Venezuelan households. This information can be used to analyze the health and food security level of the Venezuelan people in the midst of the ongoing economic collapse and food crisis. In this article, I will provide a data-driven perspective on this humanitarian crisis.

Project by Reid Thompson
